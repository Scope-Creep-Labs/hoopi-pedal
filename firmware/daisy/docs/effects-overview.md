# Hoopi Effects Overview

Hoopi is a dual-channel guitar and vocal effects processor with 8 built-in effects. Connect your guitar to the left input and a microphone to the right input for simultaneous processing.

---

## Effects

### Galaxy Reverb
A lush, studio-quality algorithmic reverb based on a Feedback Delay Network (FDN). Perfect for adding space and depth to both guitar and vocals.

- **Size** - Room size from intimate to cavernous
- **Decay** - How long the reverb tail lasts
- **Damping** - High frequency rolloff for warmer tones
- **Mix** - Blend between dry and wet signal
- **Input Gain L/R** - Independent gain control for each channel

*Both guitar and vocals are processed through the same reverb for a cohesive sound.*

---

### CloudSeed Reverb
An advanced algorithmic reverb with 8 carefully designed presets ranging from subtle room ambience to infinite, ethereal soundscapes.

**Presets:**
- Flanger Chorus - Modulated shimmer
- Dull Echoes - Dark, distant reflections
- Hyperplane - Infinite sustain
- Medium Space - Versatile room sound
- Hallway - Tight early reflections
- Rubi Ka Fields - Expansive ambient wash
- Small Room - Intimate studio vibe
- 90s Rack - Classic digital reverb character

*Both channels receive the same lush reverb treatment.*

---

### AmpSim
Neural network-powered amp modeling that captures the essence of classic amplifiers. Your guitar gets authentic tube amp tones while your vocals receive a smooth reverb.

**Amp Models:**
- Fender '57 - Clean sparkle
- Matchless - Boutique chime
- Klon - Transparent overdrive
- Mesa IIC+ - High-gain lead
- Bassman - Warm vintage clean
- 5150 - Modern metal crunch
- Splawn - Aggressive rock tones

*Guitar: Amp simulation | Vocals: GalaxyLite reverb*

---

### NAM (Neural Amp Modeler)
Next-generation amp captures using Neural Amp Modeler technology. Features a curated collection of 10 amp profiles with built-in 3-band EQ for tone shaping.

**Amp Profiles:**
- Mesa Boogie
- Matchless DC30
- Dumble High Gain
- Dumble Low Gain
- Ethos
- Splawn Nitro
- PRS Archon
- JCM800
- SansAmp
- BE-100

*Guitar: NAM amp model + EQ | Vocals: GalaxyLite reverb*

---

### Distortion
Six distinct distortion flavors from subtle grit to full-on fuzz. Shape your tone with the built-in tilt EQ.

**Distortion Types:**
- Hard Clip - Aggressive, in-your-face distortion
- Soft Clip - Smooth, tube-like saturation
- Fuzz - Vintage silicon fuzz tones
- Tube - Warm valve overdrive
- Multi-Stage - Cascaded gain stages
- Diode Clip - Asymmetric clipping character

*Guitar: Distortion | Vocals: GalaxyLite reverb*

---

### Delay
Versatile delay with four unique modes. From classic slapback to experimental reverse and octave effects.

**Delay Modes:**
- Forward - Classic digital delay
- Reverse - Backwards echoes
- Octave - Pitch-shifted repeats
- Reverse Octave - Combined reverse + octave

**Parameters:**
- Time - Delay time up to 2 seconds
- Feedback - Number of repeats
- Mix - Wet/dry balance
- Tone - Shape the delay repeats

*Guitar: Delay effect | Vocals: GalaxyLite reverb*

---

### Tremolo
Classic amplitude modulation with five waveform options. From subtle vintage throb to choppy modern stutter.

**Waveforms:**
- Sine - Smooth, classic tremolo
- Triangle - Gentle rise and fall
- Saw - Asymmetric pulse
- Ramp - Reverse saw pattern
- Square - Rhythmic chop

**Parameters:**
- Rate - Speed of modulation (0.5 - 15 Hz)
- Depth - Intensity of the effect
- Mod Rate - Secondary modulation for movement

*Guitar: Tremolo | Vocals: GalaxyLite reverb*

---

### Chorus
Rich stereo chorus for shimmering, dimensional tones. Adds width and movement to your sound.

**Parameters:**
- Wet - Amount of chorus effect
- Delay - Base delay time for modulation
- LFO Freq - Speed of the modulation
- LFO Depth - Intensity of pitch variation
- Feedback - Resonance and depth

*Both guitar and vocals receive stereo chorus processing.*

---

## Input Processing

All effects include a professional input processing chain for the guitar input:

### 3-Band EQ
Shape your tone before it hits the effects. Disabled by default, controllable via the companion app.

- Low band: 50 - 500 Hz
- Mid band: 250 Hz - 4 kHz
- High band: 2 - 10 kHz
- Each band: -12 to +12 dB

### Compressor
Smooth out dynamics and add sustain. Three modes via the toggle switch:

- **Clean** - No compression
- **Comp** - Gentle 4:1 compression
- **Comp+Gate** - Compression with noise gate for tight, quiet performance

---

## Dual-Channel Design

Hoopi is designed for singer-guitarists and duo performers:

- **Left Input**: Guitar with full effects processing
- **Right Input**: Microphone with dedicated reverb

For reverb effects (Galaxy, CloudSeed), both channels share the same lush reverb. For all other effects, your vocals receive a clean, optimized GalaxyLite reverb while your guitar gets the selected effect.

---

## Outputs

- **Main L/R**: Mono or stereo output to your amp or PA
- **Recording L/R**: Stereo output to the companion device for recording
